Impressions Coep

Impressions is the national-level, annual cultural festival of CoEP Tech. This fest saw its inception in 2016, started with the motto – ‘By the artists, for the artists. Our belief here at Impressions is to provide young artists with a platform that is culturally diverse, artistically creative, and respectfully gracious. Impressions is a completely student-organized fest and in its 7th edition Impressions is all set to outdo itself through unique events, and proshows. Impressions'22 takes forward the theme " प्रवाह : An Artistic Journey" and comprises of 6 modules with a total of 24 events across the multifaceted domains of art and culture. We aim to give artists a stage to portray their art and showcase their talents and skills. Impressions receives a footfall of more than 20,000 every year. And with a steady rise in footfall every year, Impressions continues to foster numerous international collaborations with artists across the globe - organizing various workshops, proshows, events, talks with eminent personalities like Subodh Bhave, Pankaj Tripathi, alongside concerts by Anuv Jain, KK, and many more. Impressions, like every year, is revving up with eagerness to achieve fresh heights and achieve a goal of having an even larger community of like-minded individuals living in the creative verse. We cordially invite you to this extravaganza and let out the artists in yourselves! Save the dates this December 22nd , 23rd and 24th in the CoEP campus! For any queries DM us on Instagram
